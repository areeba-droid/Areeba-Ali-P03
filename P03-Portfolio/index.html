Branding Guidelines

  • This site will be an e-commerce site for a company that sells tote bags, shoulder bags and/or flowers.
  • The site’s target audience is young adults in the age range of around 17-28 years old.
  • This company is appealing because of the stylish and trendy bags for affordable prices, and all the products are handmade so quality is guaranteed.

User Persona

Alice Walter - aged 20: Alice is a university student, who is very passionate about fashion and staying on top of trends. Most of the time, she can't afford all the expensive items she loves, since Alice works a minimum wage part-time job and mostly saves money for school and household expenses. Alice loves to shop on this website because the products are affordable, handmade and environmentally friendy.
